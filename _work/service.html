Customer Service

Here you will find the answers to your questions about shopping this site, your order, return policy and more.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are my shipping options?

Books purchased in Canada are shipped via Canada Post expedited parcel (2-8 business days).

Shipping times are approximate and may vary due to inclement weather, inventory volumes, and postal holidays, however if you have not received your books in a reasonable time period, please contact us and we'd be happy to help you.

I live in the Burnaby area. Can I pick up my order at the BLUE Warehouse?

Yes! Please give us a call at (604)123-4567 to place your order without a shipping charge and arrange for pickup.

What is your return policy?

We offer a full refund, less shipping and a 30% restocking fee for all books returned within 30 days for shipment. Books must be returned in excellent condition, with an unbroken spine and no visible signs of wear. The purchaser is responsible for paying return shipping to BLUE.

Damaged/Incorrect Shipment?

Please contact our Customer Service team immediately at (604)123-4567 and we'd be happy to replace your shipment free of charge.
