Policy

There are no refunds on textbooks (course books), only exchanges. Exchanges must be requested within five working days of purchase date and accompanied by the original receipt.

Books and gifts may be returned if unused, however, are subject to a 10 percent restocking charge.

No refunds/exchanges for special orders (books and non-book merchandise); sale books and merchandise (red stickers); marked books or worn merchandise; any opened packages and computer diskettes (unless merchandise is defective); clothing that has been worn or washed; art paper and writing instruments. Exchange only on Chartpack lettering.
