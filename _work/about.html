About BLUE

BLUE began as a bricks and mortar book store in 1991 in Burnaby, Canada, and has now grown to be an authoritative online resource for national and international readers.

Our clients are as diverse as our titles, including individuals, students, Fortune 500 companies, non-profits, educational institutions and anyone who needs a good read quickly and cost effectively. Aside from paper covered books, we offer eBooks for readers whom like read on their digital devices. Local and global newspapers are also available to subscribe through BLUE. Our Gifts section also provides a wide variety of choices that will make your loved ones love you more!

We offer fast and easy online shopping, direct shipping to your home or office.

Our head office is located in Burnaby, Canada, ensuring you'll always get the best books quickly and easily.
